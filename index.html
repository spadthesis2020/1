The Department of Architecture, School of Planning and Architecture, New Delhi is a nucleus
of young designers thriving for knowledge and innovation. Each student is molded to emerge
as an asset for the industry through active participation and perseverance. The students
analyze and address a problem from multiple perspectives, empathizing with the end user. The
strong design sensibility is inculcated within the students through a holistic approach, and
an endeavour to improve the quality of life
